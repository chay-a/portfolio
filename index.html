VO!D

VO!D is a first person platform/reflection game where you can launch a ball and teleport next to it. The goal is to solve puzzles so you can explore several levels.
This game made with Unity was created by 7 people in 15 days, and includes 6 playable levels for about 20 minutes of play time.
